Play Table Tennies (Ping Pong as we call in the USA) till I fall off!

I have been playing this sport for some time now and love playing it whenever I get the time & opportunity. I use it as a stress buster when I'm drowned in work or studies. I feel it has helped me improve my level of concentration, focus and keeps me on my toes. It is a really popular sport and one could find it in universities and work places. I also feel it is a good opportunity for me to learn from the Chinese(who are best at it) and try to get better. I seldomly play against strangers and make new friends in the process. I'd love to play a game with you too!
