About Me

Hi! I'm Alana. I'm currently a quality assurance engineer at Amazon, where I have a rip-roaring time breaking things on the Digital Products team. It's a pretty sweet gig.

I play the alto and tenor recorders, I am awesome at word puzzles, I like climbing things, and live in Somerville, MA. When I'm not eating, sleeping, or inventing a new adventure, I love to read anything I can get my hands on, play TF2, indie games, and Go, discover interesting new food and drink, as well as watch Game of Thrones, Gravity Falls, Archer, and Futurama.
